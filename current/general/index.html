I want to pitch/present

Pitches

We will be using an interoperable, cross-platform, intuitive, multilingual framework called Paper for listing those who wish to pitch an idea or describe their NHS IT problem. Simply write your name/team name and brief title or description of the idea on the flip charts provided.

Following on from its successes at previous NHS Hack Days, Paper is scalable, easy to configure, supports a range of UX options, while being at least as water-resistant as other technical solutions. Load balancing is activated OOTB with Paper, but please avoid a DDoS by queuing nicely.

Guidelines for pitching

  • Strict time limit of 2 minutes (depending a little on numbers).
  • No AV materials please - there simply isn't time.
  • You don't need to have thought of the solution in order to pitch a problem - just pitch to the room, which is full of experience and intelligence, and see if someone can help you come up with the solution.
  • When signup closes we'll randomise the running order and project it on the screen so everyone will know what's happening.

Presentations

If you want to do a presentation of your project, please fill in (the google form)[http://goo.gl/forms/4vVZ1g1aQb] A link to a Google Form will be placed here on Sunday for you to enter your work into the presentations.

Guidelines for presentations

  • Strict time limit of 3 minutes presentation, with 2 minutes for questions (subject to change depending on numbers)
  • Please submit slides in advance with the form above. Try to give us something useful at a URL we can link to when talking about your project
