Geography

Here's the link to the venue Ziferblat Edge Street, Norther Quarter, Manchester and a map:

We'll be using their huge Sitting Room and Studio areas, it's a very varied setting with loads of different options for collaborating in small or large groups.

The pitches and presentations, as well as the welcome and closing sessions, will be in the Sitting Room.

Ziferblat is on the 1st floor of the building, but it has its own dedicated lift and is fully accessible.

Schedule

Saturday September 12th

  • 0830: Registration at the desk as you enter Ziferblat, and pitch submissions (if you are pitching an idea).
  • 0930: Opening welcome, project pitches (2 minutes each).
  • 1030: Coffee, discussion, organically form project teams and start working!
  • 1300: Lunch.
  • 1400: Continue hacking, working, creating, discussing, or whatever.
  • 1800: Pub.
(Please note this is not an overnight hack.)

Sunday September 13th

  • 0900: Arrival.
  • 0930: Continue work on project(s).
  • 1300: Lunch.
  • 1430: Hard deadline for presentation submissions.
  • 1500-1700: Presentations, awards, Thanking Of The Sponsors, closing.
