NHS Hack Day 10: London

This page gives some details for this weekend.


Contact details, questions, problems

Have a look at our FAQs or ask us questions on Twitter at @NHSHackDay!

Anything and everything else to helen at deckofpandas dot co dot uk, @DeckOfPandas

The hashtag for the weekend is #nhshd.


Geography

We'll be split across three rooms in the Henriette Raphael building: the Function Room, the Boardroom and the Revelstokes Room. There will be lunch, tea and coffee in the Function Room, so make sure you pop back every so often if you're working somewhere else.

The pitches and presentations, as well as the welcome and closing sessions, will be in the Function Room.


Schedule

Saturday May 16

  • 0830-0930: Registration and pitch submissions
  • 0930-1030: Welcome, project pitches
  • 1030- : Form project teams and start work!
  • <1300>: Lunch
  • 1800-exhausted: Socialising / dinner / sleeps
(Please note this is not an overnight hack.)

Sunday May 17

  • 0830-0930: Registration and arrival
  • 0930-: Work on project(s)
  • <1300>: Lunch
  • 1430: Hard deadline for presentation submissions
  • 1500-1730: Presentations, awards, closing
