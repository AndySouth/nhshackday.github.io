Geography

Here is a pdf map of Guy's Campus. We're in the Henriette Raphael building (F on the map).

We'll be split across three rooms in the HR building: the Function Room, the Boardroom and the Revelstokes Room. There will be lunch, tea and coffee in the Function Room, so make sure you pop back every so often if you're working somewhere else.

The pitches and presentations, as well as the welcome and closing sessions, will be in the Function Room.
