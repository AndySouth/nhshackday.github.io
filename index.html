The NHS Hack Day team run weekend events that bring together healthcare and technology professionals and other “geeks who love the NHS” to promote health, good practice and good science.

Our events are free to attend, and have great support from across the healthcare and tech sectors. We’re a friendly bunch, so come along and get stuck in!
