'''APIs''' * V.sexy comprehensive health dbs with apis http://www.freebase.com/view/medicine * Assortment of APIs and other resources assembled, mainly clinical knowledge resources http://www.openclinical.org/dld_asbruInterpreter.html * Pubmed Central Open Access Subset http://pmc.jensenlab.org/ * NHS Choices APIs can be applied for via: http://www.nhs.uk/aboutNHSChoices/professionals/syndication/Pages/Webservices.aspx NHS Choices will supply temporary APIs for hack days. How to videos can be found here: http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLnhASgDToTktsqXPnXgeK7Y0BL1dvPq-k. You can get the following from the API: * Editorial content about conditions and treatments and healthy living (including videos and animations) * Behind the Headlines (debunking dodgy news stories: http://www.nhs.uk/News/Pages/NewsIndex.aspx) * Data about where and what organisations and services are out there * Performance and facilities data about organisations and services * Comments and ratings information about services * Comments and ratings capture components (collect them on your platform and they will be fed back through to nhs.uk) * Infectious diseases links CDC: http://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/ WHO:http://www.who.int/csr/don/en/index.html HPA:http://www.hpa.org.uk/NewsCentre/ MDTravel: http://mdtravelhealth.com/recent_health_alerts.php Health Map:http://healthmap.org/en/ * Patient Opinion - Email info@patientopinion.org.uk for an api key http://www.patientopinion.org.uk/info/api via James Munro of @patientopinion * APIs for 'SNOFyre' a demonstrator tool for use with [[http://www.connectingforhealth.nhs.uk/systemsandservices/data/uktc/snomed|SNOMED CT]]clinical records in their aggregation and analysis - give a range of functions such as browsing the terminology * 'SNOB' A server for the 300,000 clinical terms found in [[http://www.connectingforhealth.nhs.uk/systemsandservices/data/uktc/snomed|SNOMED CT]] with a range of services, Beta. * [[http://bioportal.bioontology.org|BioPortal]]. A quick way to support auto-complete on a problem list app for example. `http://rest.bioontology.org/bioportal/search/?query={search term}&ontologyids=2040&maxnumhits=5&apikey={apikey}` . Need to register with http://bioontology.org for an API key. Uses SNOMED-CT Core subset. * ALISS (Access to Local Information to Support Self-management - Scottish info) - community assets of all sorts to help you live well with a Long-Term Condition. Documentation at [[http://aliss-engineclub.readthedocs.org/]] and some simple demo publishing devices at [[http://demo.aliss.org/]].
